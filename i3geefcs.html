Back I3GEE - Feedback Control Systems - FCS - 2023

This course introduces the design of feedback control systems as applied to a variety of mechanical and electrical systems. Topics include the properties and advantages of feedback systems, time-domain and frequency-domain performance measures, stability and degree of stability, the Root locus method, Nyquist criterion, frequency-domain design, and state space methods.
